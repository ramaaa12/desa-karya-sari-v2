Karya Sari Village is located in XYZ District, ABC Regency, Bali Province. Situated in a rural area with a cool tropical climate and moderate rainfall, the village is surrounded by rice fields, small forests, and rivers that support the agrarian lifestyle of its community.

üìú History and Origins

The name "Karya Sari" comes from the words "karya," meaning effort, and "sari," meaning essence or purity. Legend has it that the village was founded by a group of farmers who wanted to create a community that lived in harmony with nature and culture. These values have been preserved and passed down through generations.

üåø Natural Potential of Karya Sari Village

Karya Sari Village has abundant and diverse natural resources, including plantations, agriculture, and livestock. Rubber and palm oil plantations are among the community's mainstays, thriving on village land and managed traditionally. Additionally, food crops such as corn, cassava, and legumes are cultivated as local food sources. In livestock, residents raise cows, goats, buffaloes, and poultry to meet daily needs while supporting traditional activities. Other plantations such as coffee, coconut, and cloves are also developed to enhance the village's economic value. All these potentials are managed sustainably, making Karya Sari Village a productive rural area rich in natural resources.

External conditions in the village, such as the development area work unit at the sub-district level, are also considered.
