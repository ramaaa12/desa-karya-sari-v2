🌿 Tourism Activities in Karya Sari Village

Explore the uniqueness of nature, culture, and educational tourism together with the local community.

🌾 Rice Field Trekking

Experience walking along the rice field dikes while learning about the historic Subak irrigation system. Perfect for families and nature photography enthusiasts.

🍯 Palm Sugar Making

Join the process from tapping the sap to molding palm sugar in the traditional way. This activity is very popular among students and educational tourists.

🎨 Bamboo Handicrafts

Learn directly from local artisans to make bamboo crafts and unique village products you can take home as souvenirs.

🏡 Homestay & Culinary

Enjoy a night at a comfortable local homestay and have breakfast with a view of the rice fields and fresh air.

✨ Who Is It For?

Our activities are suitable for individuals, families, communities, and students. Every visitor has the chance to interact directly with locals, creating a warm and meaningful experience.

👤 Individual

Perfect for those who want to relax, learn about culture, or simply enjoy the village nature on their own.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family

Enjoy quality time with your family through educational and fun outdoor activities.

🤝 Community

Group activities, gatherings, or community outings become more exciting and meaningful with the villagers.

🎒 Students

Learn directly from nature and local culture, perfect for study tours, community service, or school activities.
