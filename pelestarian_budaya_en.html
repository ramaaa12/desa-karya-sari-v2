Cultural Preservation

Preserving the values of tradition and local culture for a future rooted in wisdom.

🕯️ Traditional Ceremonies

Karya Sari Village regularly holds traditional ceremonies such as Maturan Alit and Nuanan Gambuh, which are rich in spiritual meaning and Balinese life philosophy.

🎭 Arts & Local Crafts

From traditional dance performances to bamboo and wood carving, villagers actively pass on these skills to the younger generation through community training and joint activities.

🌱 Commitment to Sustainability

Cultural preservation in Karya Sari not only maintains traditions but also promotes sustainable tourism and empowers the local community economically and socially.

🌾 Cultural Education

Programs for learning culture and arts for village children, teenagers, and tourists who want to participate.

🤝 Community Collaboration

Activities such as mutual cooperation, training, and cultural festivals involving all layers of the community.

🌱 Sustainable Tourism

Developing eco-friendly tourism that supports the local economy without compromising traditions.
