Karya Sari Village Profile

Explore the authenticity of culture and nature in a sustainable tourism village.

About the Village

Karya Sari Village is a tourism village located in the rural area of Bali. With green rice fields, fresh air, and a peaceful lifestyle, it is an ideal destination for travelers seeking tranquility and authentic experiences.

Tourist Activities

Visitors can enjoy meals with rice field views, make bamboo crafts, and join the process of making palm sugar and kolang-kaling directly with the locals.

Cultural Preservation

Traditional ceremonies such as Maturan Alit and Nuanan Gambuh are preserved and carried out from generation to generation. The village is also committed to developing sustainable and eco-friendly tourism.
